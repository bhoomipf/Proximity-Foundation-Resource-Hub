📋 Golden Rules
✓Always try warm intro before cold outreach
✓Research each org's CSR report before every call
✓Max 5 new orgs contacted per month
✓Offer free Pilot Session early — removes biggest adoption barrier
✓Impact report within 48 hrs of every session — no exceptions
✗Do not bulk-email — every message must be personalised
✗Max 2 follow-ups per contact — then mark as cold
✗Never describe Proximity as "small" — describe it as "focused"

LMS Development Track
A Guided Learning Overlay for cohort-based, job-readiness training in regional languages. Zero direct cost — pro bono dev team. Full LMS team hired in April; architecture and build begins in May once the team is in seat.

What We're Building

  • Multi-source video curation (YouTube + volunteer content)
  • Quiz overlay mid-video — forced participation
  • Progress blocking until answers submitted
  • Instruction layer in Hindi / Marathi (not just English)
  • Volunteer mentor dashboard + cohort progress heatmap
  • Job-readiness tracks — not academic orientation
  • Volunteer-produced local content support

Build Timeline

APR
Full LMS team hired — Lead, Content Developer, Curriculum Reviewer. Pro bono dev team (3–4 engineers) confirmed.
MAY
Architecture scoped. Build begins — video player + quiz overlay + Hindi instruction layer + progress blocking built together. Target: vanilla MVP by end of May or early June.
JUN
Vanilla MVP launches with Cohort #2 learners. Content curation library growing. Mentor dashboard live. Baseline captured.
JUL
Iterate on learner data. Leaderboard + progress heatmap added. Completion rate vs Khan Academy being tracked.
AUG
Iterate on user data. Add 2nd language. Compare completion rates vs Khan Academy baseline.
SEP
Board-level validation report. 30% lift validated or lessons documented. Phase 2 decision.
